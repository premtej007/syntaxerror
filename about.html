    About Us

    All of us have at sometime or the other experienced difficulties communicating with people coming from varied backgrounds. This happens as India is a country of many languages. We aim to bridge the linguistic barriers that exist between communities. Also, this website also wishes to teach the languages to people who want to learn it.
